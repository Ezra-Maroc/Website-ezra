Phase 0: Your Strategic Diagnosis

Indicative duration: 1 to 3 weeks

Main Objective

Precisely assess your eligibility for Moroccan nationality and define the optimal strategy before further investment.

Our Key Actions

  • Initial Analysis: Thorough review of provided documents.
  • Detailed Interview: Understanding your family background and ties to Morocco.
  • Info Gathering: Structured collection of personal and family information.
  • Legal Assessment: Comparing your case against the Nationality Code.

Your Key Deliverable

You receive a complete eligibility report including:

  • Clear legal positioning of your case.
  • Transparent assessment of your actual chances.
  • Indicative list of essential documents to gather.

Phase 1: Rigorous File Compilation

Indicative duration: 1 to 4 months

Main Objective

Assemble a complete, rigorously verified file perfectly aligned with the requirements of Moroccan authorities.

Our Key Actions

  • Document List: Providing a personalized and exhaustive list.
  • Collection Support: Proactive assistance in obtaining required documents (Morocco & abroad).
  • Translation Management: Coordination of necessary sworn translations.
  • Legalization Tracking: Handling authentication procedures (Apostille, etc.).
  • Legal Verification: Meticulous review of each document's compliance.
  • Methodical Assembly: Organizing the file according to administrative standards.

Your Key Deliverable

A finalized file, ready for submission, containing:

  • All required and validated original documents.
  • All compliant sworn translations.
  • Appropriate legalizations and apostilles.
  • Clear organization facilitating review.
  • A precise inventory of contents for tracking.

Phase 2: Optimized Submission & Initial Follow-up

Indicative duration: 2 to 3 months

Main Objective

Ensure compliant submission of your file and initiate immediate and rigorous administrative follow-up.

Our Key Actions

  • Targeted Submission: Submitting to the most relevant competent authority.
  • Submission Confirmation: Obtaining and verifying official proof of submission.
  • Active Monitoring: Implementing regular follow-up on the progress status.
  • Investigation Management: Special attention during the security investigation phase.
  • Total Responsiveness: Quick and precise handling of any additional administrative requests.

Your Status

Your file is officially registered and actively tracked, you will have:

  • Your reference file number.
  • Confirmation of administrative admissibility.
  • A forecast of the next steps.
  • Established contact point with the reviewing services.

Phase 3: Support Until the Final Decision

Indicative duration: 7 to 20 months

Main Objective

Efficiently navigate the review phase and assist you until obtaining a favorable decision.

Our Key Actions

  • Continuous Follow-up: Periodic checks and reminders if necessary.
  • Strategic Interventions: Preventive actions to avoid potential blockages.
  • Appointment Preparation: Logistical assistance and preparation for your interview.
  • Final Appointment Assistance: Presence of a partner lawyer by your side at the court.
  • Certificate Securing: Help in retrieving the final official document.

Your Achievement

The official recognition of your status: obtaining your Moroccan certificate of nationality.

Phase 4: Administrative Finalization (Optional)

Indicative duration: 1 to 3 months

Main Objective

Facilitate Moroccan administrative procedures following the acquisition of your nationality.

Our Key Actions

  • Identity Guidance: Clear advice for obtaining your Moroccan documents (CNIE, passport).
  • CNIE Support: Specific assistance for national ID card formalities.
  • Passport Help: Guidance through the passport application process.
  • Admin. Orientation: Advice on other useful steps (consulate registration, etc.).
  • Settling-in Advice: Resources for those considering living in Morocco.

Your Full Integration

Full enjoyment of your citizenship with the acquisition of your official Moroccan identity documents.
