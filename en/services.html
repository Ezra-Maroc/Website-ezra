Our Dedicated Services

Simplify your journey to Moroccan citizenship. Ezra Maroc transforms administrative complexity into a serene and successful process, reconnecting you to your heritage.

Reconnecting with your Moroccan roots through nationality is a powerful project, but administrative procedures can often seem daunting.

At Ezra Maroc, we fully understand these challenges. That's why we've designed a comprehensive and integrated range of services, led by dedicated experts. Our mission: to transform administrative complexity into a clear, controlled, and serene journey for you.

Let us handle the technical and administrative aspects, so you can focus on what matters most: your reconnection with Morocco. Discover below how we guide you towards success, step by step.

Strategic Eligibility Assessment

What we do

  • In-depth analysis of your family situation and ties to Morocco
  • Preliminary review of the documents you possess
  • Personalized interview to understand your family history
  • Targeted genealogical research if necessary
  • Expert evaluation of your application's chances of success

Your clear benefit

Receive a frank and precise analysis of your eligibility. Decide confidently whether to proceed or not, *before* committing time and expense.

Rigorous File Compilation

What we do

  • Precise establishment of the exhaustive document list for your case
  • Proactive assistance in collecting official documents
  • Complete organization of required sworn translations
  • Meticulous management of legalization and apostille procedures
  • Rigorous legal verification of each document
  • Structured preparation of a file meeting Moroccan requirements

Your direct benefit

Ensure you submit a meticulously prepared file that is strictly compliant with authorities' requirements, thereby maximizing your chances of swift approval.

In-Depth Genealogical Research

What we do

  • Targeted investigation in civil status archives (Morocco and abroad)
  • Analysis of Adoul and consular registers
  • Reconstruction of family lines to establish lineage
  • Search for crucial documents, old or missing
  • Collaboration with local experts in archival research

Your essential proof

Find the crucial documentary evidence, even old records, to irrefutably establish your line of descent and your ancestors' nationality.

Active Administrative Follow-up

What we do

  • Optimized submission of your file to the competent authorities
  • Continuous and regular monitoring of your application's progress
  • Targeted interventions with Moroccan administrations
  • Swift handling of any additional requests
  • Transparent communication on the progress status

Your peace of mind

Gain serenity through proactive monitoring that streamlines the processing of your application and keeps you informed of progress in real time.

Post-Obtainment Support (Optional)

What we do

  • Practical advice for obtaining your Moroccan identity documents
  • Specific assistance for CNIE-related procedures
  • Complete support for the Moroccan passport application
  • Administrative guidance for post-nationality formalities
  • Sound advice for potentially settling in Morocco

Your facilitated integration

Finalize your integration as a Moroccan citizen hassle-free. We guide you to quickly obtain your official documents (CNIE, passport).

Our Integrated Approach

Each of our services is part of a comprehensive, turnkey approach. We handle all administrative and legal procedures, allowing you to focus serenely on your project of reconnecting with your Moroccan roots.

Our dedicated team coordinates all stakeholders and steps, ensuring a smooth, transparent, and stress-free experience, even in the most complex situations.
